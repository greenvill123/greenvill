Types of Personal Loans

Choose the type that matches your need and repayment capacity.

Term Loan

A fixed loan amount repaid over a set tenure with regular monthly EMIs. Best for large planned expenses like weddings, home renovation, or education.

Flexi Loan

Withdraw funds as and when needed from an approved limit. Pay interest only on the amount utilised — ideal for irregular or ongoing expenses.

Instant Loan

Pre-approved or minimal-doc loans with near-instant disbursal. Best suited for urgent financial needs where speed is critical.

Short-term Loan

Loans with a tenure of 12 months or less. Higher EMIs but lower total interest — designed for quick liquidity needs with fast repayment ability.

Pension Loan

Special personal loan schemes for retired individuals. Eligibility is based on pension income rather than active employment salary.

Travel Loan

Financing specifically for domestic or international travel. Covers flights, hotels, visa fees, and travel packages in one loan.

Repayment Period Options

Understanding tenure helps you choose the right EMI vs total cost balance.

Short-term (up to 12 months)

Higher EMI, lowest total interest. Best when you want to repay quickly and have high income.

Medium-term (1–5 years)

Balanced EMI and total cost. Most popular choice for personal, wedding, and home renovation loans.

Long-term (5–8 years)

Lowest EMI, higher total interest. Best when you need a large amount but want manageable monthly payments.
